About Me

I am a full stack web developer based in Austin, TX. I spent the last 7 years teaching 5th Grade Bilingual Math and Science. I learned a lot of skills in teaching that I believe will transfer to other work environments. I work well on a team and am comfortable splitting a project into tasks. My years in the classroom have taught me to be patient and understanding, and I handle stressful situations calmly. I have strong verbal and written communication skills and can speak with concerned parents (or customers) confidently.


I am experienced in front-end development using HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I also have experience in server-side development and MySQL and MongoDB databases. I enjoy using React and can build MERN Stack applications. I have found that I like working on projects that are related to public health and education.
